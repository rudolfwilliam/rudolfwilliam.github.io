Biography

I graduated from Highschool in Erlangen, Germany at the Gymnasium Fridericianum in 2016. During the time of my schooling, I have spent six months in Portsmouth, R.I., USA (2013) and eleven months in Trondheim, Norway (2017). I completed a B. Sc. in Computer Science at TU Dresden in 2020. Currently, I am pursuing a M. Sc. in Data Science at ETH Zürich. For my Master's thesis, I am at the Max Planck Institute For Intelligent Systems in Tübingen supervised by Michael Mühlebach and Bernhard Schölkopf.
